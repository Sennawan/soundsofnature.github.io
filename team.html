Senarathna Wanasinghe

Director

An environmental enthusiast and dedicated advocate, the founder of this Nature Foundation is deeply committed to protecting the environment and promoting sustainable practices. Passionate about preserving biodiversity and natural habitats, they lead initiatives to combat climate change, reduce pollution, and educate communities on eco-friendly living.

Amali Agaspathirana

Director

With 14 years of experience as an architect, I have designed approximately 1,000 environmentally friendly houses, hotels, and commercial buildings. The hallmark of my work lies in creating designs that conserve energy and promote sustainability. Beyond architecture, I have actively contributed to environmental conservation efforts by offering both my time and financial support to address environmental challenges in various regions.

Anuraj Ilanga

Director

Anuraj Ilanga Wijerathna, a director of the Sounds of Nature Foundation, is an award-winning film critic. He also works as a television program producer, screenwriter, and author. Currently a student at the University of Jayewardenepura, he has contributed to several environmental documentaries as a screenwriter, director, and producer.
